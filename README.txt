Turn your Shelly Wall Display into a smart home control panel powered by Homey. The app runs a local server directly on your Homey and delivers a touch-optimized dashboard with real-time device control, sensor readings, a live energy flow diagram, camera snapshots, and PIN-protected alarm management.

Devices are organized by room or shown as a flat list. Tap to toggle, slide to dim, or drag tiles to reorder. Dark and light mode, adjustable tile sizes, and a built-in energy dashboard make it a complete wall-mounted smart home interface.
