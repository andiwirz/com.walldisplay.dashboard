Turn your Shelly Wall Display into a full smart home control panel powered by Homey. The app runs a local server on your Homey that the display connects to, giving you instant control over lights, switches, blinds, alarms, and more -- right from the wall.

Every device from your Homey setup appears as a tile on the display, organized by room or as a flat list. State changes update in real time, and tapping a tile toggles the device instantly. The dashboard also supports dimmer sliders, blind position controls, sensor readings (temperature, humidity, CO2, power), camera snapshots, and drag-and-drop tile reordering -- all optimized for touch.

The built-in Energy Dashboard shows a live flow diagram with animated connections between solar panels, the power grid, your home, and battery storage -- so you always know where your energy is coming from and where it is going.

Additional features include:
- Dark mode with automatic preference saving
- PIN protection for alarm tiles
- External sensor support (e.g. reed contacts for garage doors), shown as Open / Closed with red tile highlight
- Adjustable tile size (XS to XL) via the settings page
- Choose which devices appear on the dashboard
- Energy Dashboard can be enabled or disabled independently
- The dashboard URL also works in any browser such as Chrome or Safari -- not just on the wall display
